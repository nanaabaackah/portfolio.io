Accounts Receivable (AR)

Issuing Invoices

  • Quotes Team sends deposit invoices.
  • PMs handle follow-up invoices.
  • Finance matches invoices to unnoticed payments.

Sending Invoices

  • Invoices sent daily via Azimuth.
  • Monthly AR Statements emailed.
  • Finance manages complex invoice changes.

Collections

  • Follow-ups: emails/calls start within 60 days.
  • Escalations after 60 days.
  • AR spreadsheet updated daily.

Processing Invoices

  • Vendor invoices saved by Finance.
  • Approval sought weekly; CEO co-approves over $10,000.
  • Approved invoices entered for payment.

Credit Card Expenses

  • Employees submit receipts to Finance.
  • Balances paid monthly.

Accounts Payable (AP)

Expenses

Employee Reimbursable Expenses

  • Entered/approved by Project Managers.
  • Reimbursed bi-weekly, opposite of payroll cycle.

Project-Related Expenses

  • Vendor/subcontractor invoices sent to PM for approval.
  • Payments handled per AP process.

Bi-Weekly Payroll

  • Includes regular, overtime, PTO, and vacation hours.
  • Payroll taxes remitted weekly.

T4s and WSIB

  • T4s emailed before February 28 annually.
  • WSIB remittance due monthly before the last day.

Payroll Changes

  • New hires, promotions, and terminations processed through HR and Finance collaboration.

Payroll

Financial Reporting

Monthly Reporting

  • For internal use, shared in Financial Reports Chat Group.

Year-End Reporting

  • Prepared for external parties (RBC, CRA).
  • Financial statements required within 90 days of fiscal year end.

HST

  • Filed/paid monthly via QuickBooks and RBC.

Corporate Income Taxes

  • Filed annually by Baker Tilly KDN LLP.
  • Payments due within 2 months post fiscal year end.
